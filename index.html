What is ACSC?

The Ateneo Corporate Sustainability Conference is a two-day conference for Grade 10-12 high school students interested in learning about Corporate Sustainability! ACSC 2019 aims to highlight how businesses can contribute to sustainable development in the Philippines through solidifying participants’ notions of corporate sustainability: that businesses need not sacrifice the well-being of society and the planet for profit.

Management Engineering Association

The Management Engineering Association (MEA) is the Ateneo de Manila University's premier organization for BS Management Engineering majors. The organization’s vision is to develop competent and socially responsible business leaders and corporate managers of the future. It hopes to achieve this through advocating corporate sustainability and helping its members develop their business and management core competencies through academic support, leadership training, and project implementation. MEA has annually hosted ACSC since 2016.
