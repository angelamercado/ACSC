What is ACSC?

The Ateneo Corporate Sustainability Conference is a two-day conference for Grade 10-12 high school students interested in learning about Corporate Sustainability! ACSC 2019 aims to highlight how businesses can contribute to sustainable development in the Philippines through solidifying participants’ notions of corporate sustainability: that businesses need not sacrifice the well-being of society and the planet for profit.

Management Engineering Association

The Management Engineering Association (MEA) is the Ateneo de Manila University's premier organization for BS Management Engineering majors. The organization’s vision is to develop competent and socially responsible business leaders and corporate managers of the future. It hopes to achieve this through advocating corporate sustainability and helping its members develop their business and management core competencies through academic support, leadership training, and project implementation. MEA has annually hosted ACSC since 2016.

What is CS?

Corporate Sustainability (CS) is an alternative to the traditional growth and profit maximization models. CS recognizes that a company’s long-term development requires delivery of societal goals on financial, environmental, social, and ethical terms.

Testimonials

James Ang

BS Management Engineering, Ateneo de Manila University

To be completely honest, I attended ACSC 2017 not expecting much for it. The ACSC applications happened just a few months before the applications for that year’s college entrance tests and for me, this event was nothing more than an opportunity to get a glimpse of the culture in Ateneo and MEA. Little did I know that this experience would drastically change the way I perceive business. The event made me realise that rather than being just a medium to make money, business is an opportunity to make a positive impact on our society and environment. The thing I believe sets ACSC apart is how intentional every part of it is. From the talks to the activities, every single part of the experience was well thought of and serves a purpose. They are simple enough that I, who knew nothing about corporate sustainability, was able to understand, but also thorough enough that the whole ACSC experience was still able to leave a lasting impact on me and my fellow participants. All these things plus the wonderful people I met are what made my ACSC experience memorable enough for me to apply again the following year for ACSC 2018, and this year as a Programs core member!

ACSC 2019 will be on April 6 and 7, 2019, from 8 AM to 6 PM! It will be held at the Ateneo de Manila University, Escaler Hall.

Any Grade 10-12 (as of SY 2018-2019) student interested in learning about corporate sustainability may apply! You do not have to be in a business strand to join the conference.

ACSC 2019 includes various talks by esteemed speakers in the fields of sustainability and business, as well as many fun and exciting activities and workshops where participants can apply what they have learned!

The event is open to students from all over the Philippines. However, ACSC 2019 cannot provide you with transportation and lodging should you come from a different region. Rest assured that ACSC 2019 will definitely be worth the trip!

There is a registration fee of 600 pesos for private school students, and 400 pesos for public school students. This fee will cover your participant kit, food, and materials for the activities.

If you would like to request for subsidy, please fill up the form found here and explain your situation to the ACSC team! We would not want financial difficulty to prevent you from attending the conference.
