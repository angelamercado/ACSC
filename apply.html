How to Apply for ACSC

1. Read through the ACSC Website and FAQ!

Everything you need to know about ACSC can be found on our website. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us!

2. Fill up the application form below.

The applic

3. Submit your application form by March 3, Sunday!

Make sure to send in your form by 11:59 PM of March 3, Sunday, 2019.

4. Check your email and the ACSC Facebook page for the release of accepted participants.

Results will be released by Mid-March 2019. Rest assured that our deliberation process will be as fair as possible for all applicants, so don't worry about it!

5. To apply for subsidy, please contact the ACSC team!
